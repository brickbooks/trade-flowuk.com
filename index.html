TradeFlow

A modern booking-off system built for price-work construction workers. Create booking sheets, calculate totals, and export professional PDFs — without spreadsheets.

TradeFlow is a product developed and operated by Buildtech Solutions, a UK-based software and technology company.

Why TradeFlow

TradeFlow is designed by people who work on site. It removes paperwork friction and helps trades get paid faster with clear, professional booking-off sheets.

Fast Booking Off

Add labour lines, quantities, and rates in seconds — optimised for mobile use on site.

Automatic Totals

No more Excel formulas. Totals, plot values, and splits are calculated instantly.

Professional PDFs

Generate clean, professional PDFs ready to send to contractors or office teams.

Worker Splits

Split totals between workers, apply CIS deductions, and see net figures clearly.
